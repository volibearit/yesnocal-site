Privacy Policy

Effective: February 26, 2026 · Last updated: February 28, 2026
Your nutrition data stays on your device. We don't have servers, we don't collect your data, and we don't sell anything to anyone.

1. Who We Are

YesNoCal is an AI-powered nutrition tracking app. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data That Stays On Your Device

YesNoCal uses a local-first architecture. The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers (because we don't have servers): meal logs and nutrition data, body measurements and weight history, supplement tracking data, macro targets and dietary goals, badge and achievement progress, app preferences and settings, and your name (used only for display within the app).

3. Data Shared With Third Parties

AI Nutrition Estimation — When you log a meal using AI (camera, voice, or photo), your meal description or photo is sent to the AI provider (Google Gemini). These requests are made directly from your device. We never see or store these requests.

Barcode Lookups — When you scan a food barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org). No personal information is included.

Subscription Management — If you subscribe to YesNoCal Plus, your purchase is processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous app user ID — not your name, email, or nutrition data.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: email addresses or account credentials (there are no accounts), location data, analytics or usage tracking, advertising identifiers, contact lists, or any health data beyond what you voluntarily enter.

5. Device Permissions

Camera: To photograph meals for AI analysis. Photo Library: To select existing food photos. Microphone: Voice input for meal logging. Notifications: Supplement reminders and meal suggestions.

6. Data Export & Deletion

Export all your data as CSV from Settings. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data. You can also use "Reset Everything" in Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

YesNoCal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

9. Contact

Questions? privacy@yesnocal.com
