Privacy Policy

Effective: February 26, 2026 · Last updated: February 26, 2026

The short version: Your nutrition data stays on your device. We don't have servers, we don't collect your data, and we don't sell anything to anyone.

1. Who We Are

YesNoCal ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered nutrition tracking app. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data That Stays On Your Device

YesNoCal is designed with a local-first architecture. The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers (because we don't have servers):

  • Meal logs and nutrition data
  • Body measurements and weight history
  • Supplement tracking data
  • Macro targets and dietary goals
  • Badge and achievement progress
  • App preferences and settings
  • Your name (used only for display within the app)

3. Data Shared With Third Parties

When you use certain features, limited data is sent to third-party services:

AI Nutrition Estimation — When you log a meal using AI estimation, your meal description or photo is sent to the AI provider you've configured (such as Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, or OpenAI). These requests are made directly from your device to the AI provider using API keys you provide. We never see or store these requests. Each provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle this data.

Barcode Lookups — When you scan a food barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org), an open-source food database. No personal information is included.

Subscription Management — If you subscribe to YesNoCal Plus or Pro, your purchase is processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous app user ID — not your name, email, or nutrition data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

To be explicit, we do not collect: email addresses or account credentials (there are no accounts), location data, analytics or usage tracking, advertising identifiers, contact lists or social connections, or any health data beyond what you voluntarily enter.

5. Device Permissions

YesNoCal may request the following permissions, each used solely for the stated purpose:

  • Camera: To photograph meals for AI analysis
  • Photo Library: To select existing food photos for analysis
  • Microphone: To use voice input for meal logging
  • Notifications: To send supplement reminders and meal suggestions (only if you enable them)

6. Data Export & Deletion

You can export all your nutrition data as a CSV file at any time from Settings. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data. You can also use the "Reset Everything" option in Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

YesNoCal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at privacy@yesnocal.com.
